  • About Us

    North Toronto Painters is an independent painting company entering our fifth year of operation, and would love to make you our next satisfied customer! With 12 years painting experience between the two of us, you are getting professional quality at affordable prices.

  • Co-Founders

    Spencer Butt

    Spencer Butt graduated from the University of British Columbia with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology in May 2016. He grew up in the Lawrence Park area, attending Lawrence Park Collegiate Institute. Growing up, he often helped friends and family with painting and various other odd jobs. In the summer following freshman year, Spencer decided to create his own independent painting company. He has 6 years painting experience, including one with College Pro Painters. Spencer holds himself to the highest of standards, keeping quality high at North Toronto Painters.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is simple: leave each and every customer satisfied with a job well done. We respect your property and your vision. We complete jobs on time and on budget – punctuality with no nonsense. We pride ourselves on attention to detail and great customer service in order to provide a trustworthy and friendly painting experience. We will work within your budget to leave you happy.

  • Painting Responsibly

    At North Toronto Painters, we are committed to doing everything we can to be an eco-friendly company. From eliminating harmful plastic waste, to choosing green products, we try to do our part in keeping the Earth looking great for our generation and countless others to come. We believe that each of us has a role to play in combating climate degradation, and by taking responsibility for our decisions, we can contribute to a brighter future.
