  • Services

    We offer a wide range of professional services, both inside and outside the home. Here are just a few:

    • Interior:
      • Walls
      • Ceilings
      • Doors and trim
      • Baseboards and crown moulding in any room of the house
    • Exterior:
      • Painting and staining
      • Porches and decks
      • Fences, sheds, siding
      • Brick and Stucco
    The above services can be geared for any situation: staging, renovations, move-in, touch-ups, or just a plain old change of scenery!

  • Process

    Our process from start to finish is the result of years of experience. We aim to provide quality service each and every step of the way.
    • Prep – ensuring a good surface is the key to a smooth finish! We patch holes, fill cracks and caulk gaps, sand smooth, and prime where needed.
    • Paint – using the right paint and the best techniques to leave your property looking better than ever
    • Clean Up - we clean so you don’t have to, and leave your house looking like it did before (or maybe better)!
